Reverse Polarity Protection

/Geek Area / Electronics / Power Supply & Control / Reverse Polarity Protection

This trick using a P channel mosfet is a common way to provide reverse polarity protection on a power supply input.  It avoids the voltage drop problem of using a diode, with just the resistance of the mosfet to consider.

Important – ensure you don't violate the mosfet max Vgs and Vds specification!
